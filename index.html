What is
Over-the-Counter Trading?

Crypto OTC swapping is the direct buying and selling of cryptocurrencies between two parties without the need for a centralised or decentralised exchange, often used for large transactions due to liquidity constraints, price impact or transaction fees.

Our tokenomics and dual DEX/OTC swap setup creates an ever growing model. No buytax on the DEX/OTC buys encourages easy onboarding. A high sell tax on DEX drives smart traders to sell on our OTC swap with zero selltax.
